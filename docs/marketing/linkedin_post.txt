We said Phase 2 was coming.

It's here.

We took the pipeline we built last week and asked one question: what real problem can this solve right now?

Ghana's Communications Minister just announced a SIM re-registration exercise. Every subscriber needs to verify their identity. Millions of people. Manual queues. Room for fraud.

We built the answer.

Upload a Ghana Card. Take a selfie. In under 4 seconds the system reads your name, extracts your ID number, compares your face, and returns a verdict.

PASS. 99.92% confidence.

No agent. No counter. No queue.

And it doesn't stop at the Ghana Card — it reads general photo IDs and matches faces against live selfies in real time.

We documented the full build — the architecture, the bugs, the decisions, and what we're shipping next.
