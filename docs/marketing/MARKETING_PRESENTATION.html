VeriGH The Regulatory Moment

Ghana's Government Has Mandated Digital Identity Verification. Right Now.

The NCA Directive: Ghana's National Communications Authority, acting on instructions from the Communications Minister, has mandated SIM card re-registration using the Ghana Card. Every mobile subscriber must be biometrically verified or lose access.
The deadline is not optional. Millions of subscribers. Hundreds of service centres. The current manual process cannot keep up.
📱

40 Million

Mobile subscribers in Ghana who must be re-verified under the NCA directive

🏛️

Government-Issued Ghana Card

Issued by the National Identification Authority. Already in millions of hands, it carries a face photo and a unique PIN.

⏰

Compliance is Mandatory

Telecoms and service providers are legally obligated to enforce identity verification at point of SIM registration

VeriGH The Problem

The Current Approach Is Broken at Scale

🧍

Manual Agent Verification

A human agent physically inspects the Ghana Card, compares the photo to the customer's face, and types data into a form. Error-prone, slow, and impossible to scale.

🚨

No Face Matching. Fraud Is Undetectable.

Any person can walk into a service centre with someone else's Ghana Card. Without automated face comparison, the fraud goes completely undetected and the entire mandate is compromised.

📂

No Audit Trail

There is no tamper-proof record of who was verified, when, by which agent, or with what confidence. Regulators have no visibility. Telcos have no protection.

Minutes Average wait time per customer at service centres
0% Automated fraud detection in current manual process
None Digital audit trail from manual verifications
40M+ Subscribers to re-verify, far beyond manual capacity
Manual verification does not scale to a 40-million-subscriber re-registration exercise. The bottleneck is not the Ghana Card. It is the agent.

VeriGH Trust & Compliance

Built for Regulated Industries. Audit-Ready by Design.

🗂️

Immutable Audit Trail

Every verification, whether pass or fail, is written to DynamoDB with session ID, timestamp, confidence score, extracted name, ID number, and outcome. Regulators receive a complete compliance record.

🔐

Least-Privilege Security

IAM roles are scoped to the minimum permissions needed. No function has access to resources beyond its specific task. Security built in at the infrastructure level, not added on top.

📊

Real-Time Monitoring

CloudWatch alarms trigger the moment any Lambda function throws an error. 14-day log retention. Every API call is traceable end-to-end with full request and response context.

🌐

AWS Infrastructure

Runs on Amazon Web Services, the same infrastructure trusted by Ghana's largest banks and telecoms. 99.99% SLA. Data residency is configurable to the AWS Africa (Cape Town) region.

⚠️

Graceful Error Handling

Invalid formats, oversized files, and missing faces all return clean, actionable messages. Raw system errors are never exposed to users. Every edge case is handled and logged for investigation.

🔄

Auto-Retry on Throttle

Rekognition API throttling is handled automatically with exponential retry (3 attempts, 2-second delay). No verification fails due to a transient cloud capacity issue.
